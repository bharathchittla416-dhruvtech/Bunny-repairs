About us

Small shop. Serious standards.

Bunny Mobile Repairs started in 2019 out of a tiny kiosk with one goal: fix phones properly, explain things honestly, and charge a fair price. Six years and 12,000+ devices later, that's still the whole job description.

Every technician on the bench is certified. Every part we stock is quality-graded. And every repair walks out with a 90-day warranty — because if we're confident enough to fix it, we're confident enough to stand behind it.
